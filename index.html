FIGHT FAKE NEWS

Fake News is an inaccurate, sometimes a fabricated report that is created to gain attention, mislead or deceive people. Social media especially WhatsApp in particular has proved to be an easy venue for distributing fake news. Bogus stories can be shared or posted from a mobile smartphone and quickly distributed to a large audience through sharing.
As one can see that the world is presently facing an unprecedented challenge and is fighting against SARS-CoV 2 or in simple terms CORONA VIRUS, the spread of fake news regarding this virus is resulting in unnecessary confusion and chaos among the people. These messages may contain useless, incorrect or even harmful information and advice, which can obstruct the public health response and add to social disorder due to panic strikes. In order to avoid all these kinds of misinformation one should only try to gather information from reliable sources rather than WhatsApp forwards or posts that are being circulated.
